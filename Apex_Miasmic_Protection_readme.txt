An assortment of items used to protect the prospector from miasmic damage.

AntiMiasmic Pills can be crafted in the Medicine or Chemistry Bench and will keep miasmic damage at bay for a short while.

Alternatively, the Miasmic Satchel can be crafted at the Advanced or Electric Textiles Bench, and as the name states, it will carry miasmic items for you.

As a bonus, any miasmic items you pickup will automatically be deposited into the Miasmic Satchel.
